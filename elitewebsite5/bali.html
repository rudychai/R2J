4 Days BALI TOUR
Kintamani/ Bedugul

DAY 01 : ARRIVAL AT NGURAH RAI AIRPORT
Meeting arrival at Ngurah Airport by our representative - transfer to Hotel - free for own leisure.

DAY 02 : KINTAMANI ( B,L,D )
Kintamani tour visitting: Desa Batubulan for Barong dance show, Celuk village, handy craft and silver center, Mas Seni Ukir (Wood carving), Elephant cave and Pura Tirta Empul / Tampak Siring, where you can see the historical Palace of the first President of Indonesia (Mr.Soekarno) then proceed to Kintamani for lunch. In the afternoon free shopping at Pasar Seni Sukawati in ubud and Joger Kuta. Dinner at Jimbaran beach.

DAY 03 : BEDUGUL TOUR ( B,L,D )
After breakfast tour to the center part of Bali visitting: Sangeh Forest, where you can see and feed the monkeys. then continue to Bedugul highland with a beautiful scenerary of Lake Beratan. On the way back, we will stop over at Pura Taman Ayun Mengwi and Tanah Lot.

DAY 04 : DEPARTURE ( B )
Free until the time to transfer to the airport.
