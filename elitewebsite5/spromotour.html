7 Days HENAN TOUR
Zhenzhou/ Kaifeng/ Shaolin Temple/ Luoyang/ Mt.Yuntai tour
Day 1: Arrive Zhengzhou (D)
Meet at airport, transfer to hotel.
Day 2: Zhengzhou/ Kaifeng (B/L/D)
After breakfast, visit one of China’s biggest museums, Henan National Museum and a wonderful show of Ancient Chinese Music. Then Take bus to Kaifeng, well-famed ancient capital. Visit : the Dragon Pavilion, former site of the Forbidden City of six dynasties; Iron Pagoda built in 1049 A.D.; Millennium City - the largest Song Dynasty theme park in the world, where you can see and join various ancient folk shows; Memorial Temple to Judge Bao; Evening tour of the ancient town and try local flavors at the famous Drum Tower Night Market.
Day 3: Kaifeng/ Shaolin (B/L/D)
After breakfast, head for Mt. Songshan, tour the Shaolin Temple, Pagoda Forest and a breathtaking Shaolin Kung Fu Performance. In the evening, optional performance: Zen Music Shaolin Grand Ceremony, composed by Mr. Tan Dun, Oscar and Grammy winner who composed the music of Crouching Tiger Hidden Dragon. With an investment of 360 million Yuan RMB (360 billion Indonesia Rupiah) and an incredible audio and visual effect, this is the biggest sightseeing performance in China that you can never miss!
Day 4: Shaolin/ Luoyang (B/L/D)
After breakfast, take bus to the most famous ancient capital in Chinese history - Luoyang. Visit the World Cultural Heritage Site and the treasure house of art - Longmen Grottoes; Guanlin - tomb and memorial temple to Lord Guangong during the Three Kingdoms Period, who was famous for his loyalty and courage; Luoyang Peony Garden (April 1 to May 1).
Day 5: Luoyang/ Zhengzhou (B/L/D)
After breakfast, back to Zhengzhou, visit the Yellow River Park; The Giant Statue of Forefathers at the Forefathers Altar Square then take China’s only tourist hovercraft to tour the great mother river of the Chinese nation. Back to downtown Zhengzhou, the transportation and commercial center in Central China, also called China’s Chicago, here commerce and trade prosper, you can make a big shopping before you leave.
Day 6: Zhengzhou/ Yuntai Mountain/ Zhengzhou (B/L/D)
Visit the Yuntai Mountain World Geo-park, which is called Northern Jiuzhaigou for its grand valleys, lakes and waterfalls.
Day 7: Leave Zhengzhou (B)
Depart from Zhengzhou after breakfast.
*** Home Sweet Home ***

HARI 01 BANGKOK (D)

HARI 02 BANGKOK - PATTAYA (B/L/D)
Pagi ini acara tour dimulai dengan menyusuri sungai Chao Phraya dengan kapal boat, mengunjungi pasar terapung dan kanal Klongs. Kemudian dilanjutkan ke vihara Wat- Arun dan Wat-Po untuk melihat patung Reclining Buddha. Acara dilanjutkan ke peternakan ular terbesar di Asia dan mengunjungi Grand Palace dan selanjutnya ke Dream World, di mana anda bisa menikmati berbagai permainan yg seru dan menegangkan. Sore harinya, perjalanan ke Pattaya dengan bus.
HARI 03 PATTAYA (B/L/D)
Hari ini peserta dibawa ke Coral island (pulau karang) dengan speed boat untuk melakukan berbagai kegiatan seperti: ski air, snorkeling, menyelam , mendayung dan banyak lagi dengan biaya Anda sendiri. Setelah makan siang, acara dilanjutkan ke kampung Noong Nooch untuk menikmati pertunjukkan kebudayaan Thai berupa tari - tarian, sabung ayam, upacara pernikahan tradisional Thai, Thai Boxing dan elephant show serta benda-benda kerajinan tangan. Setelah makan malam, peserta boleh menikmati acara tambahan Tiffany atau Alcazar show atas biaya sendiri.
HARI 04 PATTAYA - BANGKOK (B/L/D)
Setelah sarapan pagi, perjalanan dilanjutkan ke Bangkok. Dalam perjalanan anda-anda mempunyai kesempatan berbelanja makanan kering, kemudian dilanjutkan ke Safari World, untuk melihat binatang buas yang hidup di alam bebas.
