7 Days LAKE TOBA / SAMOSIR Island / BUKIT TINGGI / PADANG ~ SIKUAI Island / MEDAN

Day 01 MEDAN - PARAPAT (L/D)
Arrival meeting by our representative - lunch at local restaurant - depart for lake Toba via Tebing Tinggi and stop at Pematang Siantar for visiting the famous local Peanuts candy shop - check-in hotel in Parapat town - dinner at hotel / local restaurant - free program.
Day 02 PARAPAT - SAMOSIR (B/L/D)
After breakfast a short city tour in Parapat town - drop at Pasar Siang malam, where you can see the local handicraft, wood carving, Ulos cloth, etc - lunch at local restaurant - short cruise to Tomok ( tomb of King Sidabutar ) and Ambarita ( the Palace of King Siallagan ) villages - check-in hotel in Samosir island - dinner at hotel - free program
Day 03 SAMOSIR - BUKIT TINGGI (B/L/D)
After breakfast depart from Samosir to jetty in Parapat town then proceed to Bukit Tinggi via Sidempuan & Tarutung - on arrival check-in hotel - free program - dinner at hotel.
Day 04 BUKIT TINGGI TOUR - PADANG (B/L/D)
After breakfast depart for city tour, visiting: Pasar Atas/ Bawah, Gadang's Clock, Japanese tunnel - after lunch transfer to Padang.
Day 05 PADANG - SIKUAI Island (B/L/D)
After breakfast transfer by boat to Sikuai Island - on arrival check-in hotel - Free for water sport activities such as snorkelling, swimming or fishing.
Day 06 SIKUAI Island - MEDAN (B/L/D)
Transfer back to Padang Airport for departure to Medan by domestic flight - on arrival city tour in Medan visiting Maimoen Palace, Buddha temple, Grand mosque, China town and shopping.
Day 07 DEPARTURE (B)
After breakfast Transfer to Polonia Airport for departure to next destination.
